The Source

Herbs & flowers from the
motherland, in their
purest form.

From the highlands of Kenya to the world's finest laboratories, beauty houses, and kitchens. We work directly with farming communities who have cultivated these botanicals for generations.

Africa holds the world's richest botanical heritage — yet less than 10% of its crops are processed locally. The value leaves the continent. We're changing that.

Moedim AI connects smallholder farmers growing lavender, rosemary, lemongrass, basil, thyme, chamomile, and more — directly to global buyers. We process on the continent. We certify to the highest international standards. And we ensure every farmer earns a fair, sustainable price that transforms their livelihood.

Innovation

AI-powered integrity.
From seed to shelf.

We don't just source herbs. We've built an AI-powered supply chain that ensures purity, traceability, and compliance at every step — bringing those typically left out of global trade into the agricultural supply chain using technology.

◈

AI Supply Chain

Artificial intelligence manages scheduling, quality control, inventory forecasting, and farmer-to-buyer matching. Every transaction optimized. Every kilogram tracked. Every farmer connected via WhatsApp — no app download needed.

◉

Full Traceability

Blockchain-verified chain of custody from individual farm plots to your receiving dock. Know exactly which farmer grew your lavender, when it was harvested, how it was processed, and every hand it passed through.

◊

EU & US Certified

Built for compliance from day one. COA, TDS, SDS, DOA documentation per batch. Organic certification pathway (EU Regulation 2018/848). KEPHIS phytosanitary clearance. FDA and REACH compliant.
