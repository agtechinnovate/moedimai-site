# MoedimAI products and crop families

MoedimAI supports African agricultural bioeconomy supply chains across multiple crop and product families. The workflow is configured around the buyer-ready outcome, crop, source records, farmer network, value-addition step, evidence requirement, logistics route, distribution path, and route to market.

## Carrier and cold-pressed oils

Representative products: baobab, moringa, avocado oil, sesame, sunflower, groundnut, soybean, coconut, castor, macadamia, and related carrier or oilseed lines.

MoedimAI supports grower coordination, harvest benchmarks, cold press, fatty-acid or quality evidence, custody, logistics routing, and buyer packets. Baobab, moringa, and avocado oil are carrier or cold-pressed oils, not essential oils.

## Essential oils and steam-distilled aromatics

Representative crops: lavender, rosemary, tea tree, eucalyptus, lemongrass, peppermint, basil, lippia, rose geranium, immortelle, leleshwa, and similar aromatic crops.

MoedimAI supports harvest timing, biomass readiness, steam distillation, hydrosol or essential-oil records, QC evidence, GC-MS or composition evidence where used, custody, and buyer specification checks.

## Aromatic crops and botanicals

Representative products: medicinal, cosmetic, fragrance, tea, herb, natural-ingredient, dried botanical, extract, powder, and specialty crop lines.

MoedimAI supports producer onboarding, drying or processing evidence, organic-conversion records where relevant, lot documentation, and export readiness.

## Fresh produce and horticulture

Representative crops: avocado, mango, pineapple, passion fruit, banana, citrus, papaya, French beans, peas, vegetables, and export horticulture.

MoedimAI supports farm records, stage checks, harvest readiness, grading, residue-risk workflows, cold-chain or distribution routing, and buyer movement.

## Spices and herbs

Representative crops: vanilla, ginger, turmeric, chili, black pepper, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, basil, mint, and related culinary or medicinal herbs.

MoedimAI supports smallholder sourcing, quality benchmarks, drying and processing evidence, logistics routing, traceability, and buyer matching.

## Grains, pulses, and oilseeds

Representative crops: maize, sorghum, millet, rice, wheat, beans, cowpea, pigeon pea, chickpea, lentils, sesame, sunflower, groundnut, soybean, and other oilseeds.

MoedimAI supports farmer-network management, yield benchmarking, aggregation, storage evidence, route planning, offtaker readiness, and funder reporting.

## Tree crops

Representative crops: coffee, tea, cocoa, cashew, macadamia, avocado, shea, coconut, and agroforestry tree crops.

MoedimAI supports plot records, quality and origin evidence, buyer compliance, harvest planning, processing evidence, and export documentation.

## Bioeconomy biomass crops

Representative categories: biomass crops, crop residues, bamboo, sisal, cotton, regenerative and agroforestry supply programmes, bio-based packaging feedstock, briquettes, pellets, biogas, bioplastics, compost, animal feed, and other circular value-addition routes.

MoedimAI supports production tracking, field evidence, sustainability records, biomass value-chain custody, processing, and movement into distribution.
