# Security policy

Report a suspected vulnerability through [www.moedim.ai/security](https://www.moedim.ai/security) or email `vivian@moedim.ai` with the subject **Security report**.

Do not include passwords, authentication codes, identity documents, bulk personal records, malware, or destructive payloads in ordinary email. Do not disrupt service, access another person's data, use social engineering, or retain data encountered accidentally. Stop testing once you have enough evidence to explain the concern.

Moedim records reports in its restricted incident register, prioritizes them by risk, preserves relevant evidence, and coordinates disclosure after a fix and safe release plan are established. This policy does not authorize unlawful testing or testing of systems not owned by Moedim.
